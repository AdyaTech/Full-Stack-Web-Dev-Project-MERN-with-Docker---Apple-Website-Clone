      Overview

      • Apple TV 4K (3rd generation) brings the best of TV together with your favourite Apple devices and services — including FaceTime on TV. Footnote 3 With 4K Dolby Vision, HDR10+ and Dolby Atmos, it delivers a truly cinematic experience to your screen. Footnote 4 You can enjoy content from Apple TV, Apple Music, Apple Arcade and Photos. Footnote 5
      • The included Siri Remote (3rd generation) with USB-C makes it easy to control Apple TV 4K using the touch-enabled clickpad or just your voice — and if it goes missing, you can find it fast with your iPhone. Footnote 6 Apple TV 4K also works as a smart home hub, letting you connect and control all your favourite smart home accessories. Footnote 7
      • Apple TV 4K (Wi-Fi) comes equipped with 64GB of storage, and Apple TV 4K (Wi-Fi + Ethernet) comes with 128GB of storage and Thread networking support. Footnote 8

      What you need

      • A TV with HD capability or better Footnote 4
      • HDMI cable for HD streaming, or Dolby Vision–compatible HDMI cable for 4K HDR streaming
      • Wireless network or wired Ethernet (128GB model only)
      • Broadband Internet connection (fees may apply)
