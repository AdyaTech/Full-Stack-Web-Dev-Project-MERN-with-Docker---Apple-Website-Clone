      Zero-Touch Deployment

      Setup in a snap.

      Apple Business Manager works with MDM solutions to make deployment and management exceptionally easy. Automated Device Enrolment allows your IT team to configure devices remotely and customise the setup process — so Apple devices are ready to go right out of the box.

      Device Management

      Keep it all under control.

      Apple devices come with built-in management and security frameworks that make it easy for IT to configure and manage devices, buy and distribute apps, and monitor threats remotely. Whether you have 10 devices or 10,000, IT can easily create profiles to ensure that employees have everything they need to be secure and productive.

      Hardware and Software

      Secure by design.

      Apple hardware and software are designed for maximum security. Whether your clinicians are on Mac, iPad or iPhone, Apple silicon and operating systems work together to deliver powerful features that keep your organisation’s data and devices safe.

      Touch ID and Face ID

      One touch and you’re in.

      Touch ID and Face ID give users a fast, easy and secure way to unlock devices, open protected files and retrieve passkeys at a touch or a glance. So your clinicians won’t waste time looking up forgotten passwords.
