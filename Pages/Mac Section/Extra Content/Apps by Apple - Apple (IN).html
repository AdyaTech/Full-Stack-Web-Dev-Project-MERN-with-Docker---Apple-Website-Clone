    Create any image you can dream up. Use concepts, descriptions
    and your photo library to
    create fun, playful images in multiple styles

    across apps, so
    you can use it in places like Messages to express yourself.    7

    Passwords

    The Passwords app lets you access account passwords, passkeys,
    Wi‑Fi passwords and verification codes — all in one place. It helps
    manage credentials across devices with iCloud and alerts you to
    insecure passwords. You can create, save and customise strong
    passwords, share them with others, and more.
