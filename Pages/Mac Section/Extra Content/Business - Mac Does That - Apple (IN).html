    Do my work apps run on Mac?

    They do. Run the apps your teams use every day — from Microsoft, Google, Slack, Zoom, Bigin By Zoho CRM, WhatsApp and millions more.

    Can Mac handle demanding work tasks?

    With ease. Mac is a performance powerhouse thanks to Apple silicon, helping accomplish virtually any task — even intensive AI demands — with impressive speed.

    Is Mac built for AI?

    Absolutely. With Apple Intelligence and a Neural Engine, every new Mac easily handles a variety of AI tasks.

    Will Mac work in my environment?

    Yes indeed. Devices come ready to support network access and cloud-based identity tools. And they fit easily into existing infrastructure, no matter how many devices you run.

    Will Mac make my organisation more secure?

    Definitely. Apple devices offer state-of-the-art security. Built-in frameworks enable IT to manage security system-wide at scale, while a robust set of features protects employees from malware and viruses.

    Does it cost less to use Mac than PC?

    Quite a bit. Studies show that dramatically lower support and energy costs, as well as long-lasting durability, make Mac a real money saver for businesses of any size.1

    Performance

    A workhorse for your workforce. Thanks to Apple silicon, Mac brings game‑changing power and speed to everything you do — from big data processing to machine learning tasks. So whether you’re taking on the everyday or your next monumental project, Mac can help you do virtually anything, faster.

    AI

    Built for AI. From daily tasks to LLM asks. Incredible performance, built-in privacy protections and an intuitive user experience are just a few of the reasons Mac is the best AI PC for work.

    • Integrated. And optimised. With AI‑optimised hardware, powerful software integration and a robust ecosystem of apps, Mac can crush everyday needs as well as highly demanding ones.

    • Apple silicon. Mac with Apple silicon features a Neural Engine and unified memory for efficient handling of sophisticated AI processing.

    • Privacy and security. Advanced privacy and security are built in to help protect your company‘s data. And on Mac, you can run AI on device, allowing you to keep inputs and results private.

    • A world of apps. An entire catalogue of AI-ready apps is available on Mac, iPhone and iPad, so you can boost your teams’ capabilities right away — and work seamlessly across devices.

    Battery

    Up to 24 hours of battery life. With all-day battery life, Mac won’t quit until you do. It brings the same outstanding performance whether it’s plugged in at the office or you’re working on the go.4 And with fast charging, your MacBook Air or MacBook Pro can charge up to 50% in around 30 minutes.5

    Apple Intelligence

    Apple Intelligence. Personal, private, powerful. Every Mac comes with Apple Intelligence, a personal intelligence system that helps your employees write, express themselves and get things done effortlessly. With groundbreaking privacy and security protections, it gives you peace of mind that no one else can access your company data — not even Apple.7

    • Write, refine, focus. Apple Intelligence brings system-wide Writing Tools to macOS, helping employees rewrite, proofread and summarise text with ease. Whether they’re drafting emails in Mail, capturing ideas in Notes or working in third-party apps, employees can communicate more clearly and efficiently. Intelligent notification prioritisation helps them stay focused and minimise distractions — so they can get more done with less effort.

    • The start of a new era for Siri. Siri draws on Apple Intelligence for new superpowers. With an all-new design, richer language understanding and the ability to type to Siri whenever it’s convenient, employees can interact in whatever way suits the moment. And with extensive product knowledge and the ability to tap into ChatGPT when it might be useful to answer a question, Siri helps your teams get answers, solve problems and stay productive.

    • Expressive tools for work. Image Playground makes it easy to generate original visuals based on text prompts or photos — perfect for presentations, brainstorming or storytelling. And with Genmoji, teams can create expressive, personalised emoji to add clarity, tone or a human touch to conversations. Whether collaborating in Messages or building content in apps like Freeform and Keynote, Apple Intelligence helps bring ideas to life — no design skills required.

    Great powers come with great privacy. Apple Intelligence is designed with privacy at its core. On-device processing means it understands personal context without collecting personal data — so your company’s information stays private and secure. For more complex tasks, Private Cloud Compute uses larger models running on Apple silicon servers with industry-leading protections. Your data is never stored or accessible to anyone — not even Apple.

    Mac + iPhone

    Mac + iPhone. Better together. Using Mac with other Apple devices lets you and your workforce do incredible things. Copy text, images, photos and videos on your iPhone and paste to your Mac — or vice versa. Start an email on iPhone during the commute and finish it on Mac when you get to the office. Use AirDrop to transfer files from your Mac to a co-worker down the hall. Or unlock your Mac simply by wearing your Apple Watch.

    Device Management

    Easy to manage. Zero touch — and go. You can provision a Mac in just minutes with zero-touch deployment. From configuring devices to deploying apps to monitoring threats, IT can do it all via mobile device management without interrupting your team’s work or compromising their privacy.1

    Security

    Priceless security. Free of charge. With enterprise-grade security at every level, Mac protects you from malware and viruses, keeps data encrypted and puts passwords at the tip of your finger with Touch ID. Free, automatic security updates allow everyone to stay focused on deliverables, without unexpected downtime.

    Durability + Reliability

    Designed to go the distance. Made of seriously strong, 100% recycled aluminium, Mac computers are built to last. With fewer tech problems, like glitches and crashes, your team can avoid unnecessary interruptions and stay productive.8 And free, automatic operating system updates keep your system running smoothly. So every Mac remains fast and responsive — now and into the future.

    Save with Mac

    Bottom line: Mac improves your bottom line. Mac is dramatically less expensive to maintain compared to PCs, with far fewer support tickets and reduced hardware, software and energy costs.1

    macOS

    Never pay for macOS updates. macOS is the operating system that powers every Mac. It’s simple to navigate and more familiar than you might think, with built-in productivity and accessibility features. And free, seamless updates keep things running smoothly so your team can tackle their to-dos — not IT issues.

    Apps

    Apps that help you get down to business. Mac comes loaded with powerful apps that help your teams fulfil virtually any task. And it runs the familiar apps they already use — like Microsoft 365 Copilot, Google Workspace, SAP Business Suite, The OmniPlan Productivity Suite, Bigin By Zoho CRM, WhatsApp and more. And just wait till they see how fast, smooth and snappy Slack, Zoom, Box and Google Docs can be. Still need more? There are nearly two million apps for business available on the App Store.9
