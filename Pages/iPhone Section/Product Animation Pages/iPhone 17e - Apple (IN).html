Value packed

Say hello to a good buy.

iPhone 17e comes with many of the same great features as iPhone 17 at a wallet-friendly price. Packed with the same enhanced scratch resistance of Ceramic Shield 2, the latest-generation A19 chip, a 48MP Fusion camera, fast MagSafe wireless charging, USB‑C and storage starting at 256GB. It’s a whole lot of iPhone. For a lot less.

Durable design

Designed to
go the distance.

Beautiful — and built to stay that way — iPhone 17e is designed with a durable aluminium frame and has Ceramic Shield 2 on the front, providing 3x better scratch resistance than iPhone 16e.1 Tougher than any smartphone glass, it protects your iPhone so you don’t have to sweat the dings, drops and dangs. iPhone 17e also has USB‑C for charging — and for connecting to Apple devices and a wide variety of accessories.

View in your space

3x better
scratch resistance with Ceramic Shield 21

  • Now with MagSafe.

    iPhone 17e enables wireless charging up to 15W, compared to 7.5W with iPhone 16e. That means when you need to charge, it’s easy, convenient and fast. And with built-in magnets, you can easily snap into a family of accessories including chargers, cases and more.2

  • Stunning display.

    The 15.40 cm (6.1″) Super Retina XDR display4 makes photos pop with colour and text sharp and easy to read. A new seven-layer anti-reflective coating reduces glare even in bright sunlight.

  • Face ID.

    Use Face ID to securely unlock and sign in to apps with just a glance.

  • Action button.

    A customisable fast track to your favourite feature. Long press to launch the action you want — Silent mode, Translation, visual intelligence5 and more.

  • Colours.

    iPhone 17e comes in three beautiful colours. Choose timeless black or white or a new blush-worthy soft pink.

Performance and battery life

Blasts and lasts.

Thanks to the A19 chip — the same generation that powers the iPhone 17 line-up — iPhone 17e has next-level power and performance that make everything you do feel fast and fluid, now and for years to come. The C1X modem is up to 2x faster than the C1 modem in iPhone 16e. Together with A19, it makes all the things you love — from streaming to AAA gaming — smooth and responsive. And with the leading power efficiency of Apple silicon and great battery capacity, iPhone 17e gets long battery life. Up to 26 hours of video playback so you can go, go, go all day.6

Up to 50% charge in 30 minutes with a 20W power adapter or higher7

48MP Fusion camera

Perfect your portraits.

The versatile 48MP Fusion camera on iPhone 17e has the capabilities of two advanced cameras in one. So you can capture super-high-resolution photos and theatre-worthy 4K videos with exceptional detail, or use the 2x Telephoto to zoom in with optical quality. And with next-generation portraits, you can capture frame-worthy portraits more easily than ever.

Next-generation portraits. Capture the moment in photos and turn them into beautiful portraits later by adding a background blur after the shot. You can even change the focus point to highlight subjects on the move, like kids or pets.

2x Telephoto. Zoom in on your subjects up to 2x with optical quality and keep going up to 10x.

Clean Up. Remove unwanted distractions. Available on all the latest iPhone models, it’s a photo editing tool that stays true to the original intent of the photo.8

Low-light and Night mode photography. Capture sharp, detailed and bright images with natural colours, even when it’s dark.

4K video. Record theatre-worthy 4K 60 fps video in Dolby Vision with automatic wind noise reduction. Use Audio Mix to adjust the way voices sound in your videos. And film multiple scenes in just one shot using pause and record.

So much more storage.
So many more memories.

With storage starting at 256GB — that’s 2x more than iPhone 16e and 4x more than iPhone 12 — you’ll have more space for what matters. From super-high-resolution photos to 4K videos to your favourite apps, games and more, there’s plenty of room for everything you love, now and in the future.

Helpful Apple Intelligence features like visual intelligence5 to make your everyday easier. And iOS for seamless ways to share photos and files, connect with friends and family, and more.

Recycled Material

Our approach.

Using recycled materials, like 85% recycled aluminium in the enclosure, reduces the need to mine new material, which avoids the carbon emissions and environmental impacts of mining.

 Learn more in our Product Environmental Report (PDF)

How you can help.

You can protect the earth’s precious resources by trading in, passing down or recycling devices and accessories you no longer use. You can bring them into any Apple Store for free, secure recycling. We also offer other ways to recycle, including mail-in options.

 Learn how to trade in or recycle devices

Renewable Electricity

Our approach.

Using electricity from renewable sources like wind and solar across our global supply chain — instead of fossil fuels — significantly reduces carbon emissions from manufacturing Apple products.

 Learn more in our Product Environmental Report (PDF)

How you can help.

You can extend the lifespan of your batteries and Apple products by using Optimised Battery Charging. It works by learning your charging habits and delaying the final charge — from 80% to 100% — until you are likely to need it.

 Learn more about Optimised Battery Charging

Our approach.

iPhone 17e comes in a 100% fibre-based box as part of our commitment to remove plastic from the packaging of all Apple products.

 Learn more in our Product Environmental Report (PDF)

How you can help.

New Apple products come in paper packaging that’s 100% fibre based. In most places, you can put the entire box into your household recycling bin. You can also bring Apple packaging to any Apple Store and we’ll recycle it for free.
