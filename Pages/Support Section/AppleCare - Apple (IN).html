    AppleCare

    Handled with AppleCare.

    AppleCare offers one-stop support and service for all of your Apple products — from the people who know them best. Get easy, fast repairs for accidents like drops and spills. A replacement battery when yours drops below 80% capacity. And priority care with just a chat, call or tap.1

    Get coverage

    Benefits

    Covered from every angle.

    AppleCare offers peace of mind for all your devices, with coverage inside and out.2, 3

    • Turn to us when accidents happen.

      Stay protected through life’s ups, downs and even drops. Get quick, Apple-certified repairs — many issues can be resolved the same day.4

    • Stay above water with liquid damage coverage.

      Liquid damage is covered as part of accidental damage protection. So now you won’t have to stress over spills and splashes.

    • Extended coverage, built in.

      AppleCare covers battery replacements at no extra charge if the capacity drops below 80%. Plans also include extended warranty coverage in the case of any hardware issues.

    • Connect with an expert at any time.

      Get answers to your questions and support for all your hardware and software needs. Contact us via text, the Apple Support app, phone, online or in person at the Apple Store.

    Questions? Answers.

    Most Apple products come with 1 year of hardware coverage through the limited warranty. AppleCare plans extend that hardware coverage and provide additional features like accidental damage protection and priority support.

    You can start an AppleCare+ plan when buying a new product with Apple in person. For 60 days, after a new Apple purchase, you can get a new plan through the settings app on your iPhone, iPad or Mac, or by calling 000800 1009009. Or within 60 days, you can go to an Apple Store to purchase a plan.

    AppleCare+ with Theft and Loss for iPhone is available for purchase when you buy AppleCare on your device.

    When you protect your iPad with AppleCare+, you’ll get coverage for accidental damage for one Apple Pencil and one Apple‑branded iPad keyboard. AppleCare+ also covers any in-box cables and power adapters that came with your covered Apple product.

    You can get your device repaired a few different ways. You can mail it in using a prepaid shipping label from Apple. Or you can bring it in to an Apple Store or any of the over 5,000 Apple Authorised Service Providers. Start a repair by visiting support.apple.com/en-in/repair, downloading the Apple Support app or calling your Apple Support phone number.
